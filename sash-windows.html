Traditional sash ironmongery

We supply superior quality ironmongery as standard with every sash and case window. Our carefully selected range includes traditional pulleys, sash fasteners, ring pulls, sash lifts, and simplex systems — all available in polished brass, antique brass, and black finishes.

The quality of the hardware is essential to the longevity and operation of your windows. We partner with leading manufacturers to ensure every component meets our exacting standards.

Timber — the sustainable choice

All our sash and case windows are manufactured from FSC certified timber, ensuring responsible sourcing from well-managed forests. Timber is one of the most sustainable building materials available.

  • Low embodied energy compared to aluminium or PVC
  • Carbon storage throughout the window's lifetime
  • Fully repairable and maintainable
  • Biodegradable at end of life
  • Can be recycled or repurposed
