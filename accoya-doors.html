DESIGNED TO BE BESPOKE

Each door is unique to the project

Accoya delivers excellent thermal efficiency, helping to keep homes warmer and more comfortable while reducing heat loss. Its natural resistance to rot and decay means far less maintenance compared to traditional timbers, and its impressive lifespan — backed by industry-leading warranties — gives our customers complete peace of mind.

Using a proprietary acetylation process, Accoya timber achieves unmatched dimensional stability, meaning it will not warp, twist, or shrink even in Scotland's harshest weather conditions.

  • 50-year above-ground guarantee as standard
  • Carbon-negative lifecycle — nontoxic with worldwide accreditations
  • Exceptional dimensional stability — will not warp or twist
  • Naturally rot and pest resistant
  • Excellent thermal efficiency — keeps homes warmer
  • Fully customisable paint finishes

THE MANUFACTURING JOURNEY

From the forest to your property

Accoya begins its life in responsibly managed forests, certified to the highest environmental standards. The acetylation process — a non-toxic modification using acetic anhydride — permanently changes the cell structure of the wood, making it dimensionally stable and resistant to rot, without introducing any harmful chemicals.

From there, our Edinburgh workshop takes over. Every door is manufactured to your exact drawings — no catalogue sizes, no off-the-shelf shortcuts. Our joiners shape, fit, finish, and glaze each door individually before it leaves our workshop.

The result: a door built for the Scottish climate, backed by a 50-year guarantee, and made to look exactly as you envisioned.
