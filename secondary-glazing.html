Secondary Glazing for Heritage Windows

Discreet, removable, energy-efficient. Designed for sash windows in listed buildings and conservation areas. No planning permission required.

Get a Survey Case Studies

Engineered for heritage properties

Secondary glazing is the invisible upgrade for historic sash windows. Our bespoke timber-framed secondary glazing system reduces heat loss and improves acoustic insulation while keeping your original windows intact and fully operational.

  • Reduce heat loss by 70%
  • Timber frame (no plastic)
  • Windows remain fully operational
  • Removable for cleaning and maintenance
  • Magnetic fixing technology
  • Invisible from outside
  • No planning permission required
  • Conservation area compliant

The Benefits of Secondary Glazing

Thermal Efficiency

Acoustic Insulation

Commercial secondary glazing for hotels

Cost-effective energy upgrade for listed hotel properties without disruption to guests. Improve energy ratings, reduce operating costs, and enhance guest comfort — all while preserving your building's heritage character.

From boutique hotels to luxury properties, secondary glazing is the preferred solution for heritage buildings seeking energy efficiency without compromise.
